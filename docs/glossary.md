# Glossary

This glossary defines project terms used across TRIDENT-LOB planning documents.

- `LOB`: Limit order book.
- `L1`: Best bid and ask quotes with sizes.
- `L2`: Aggregated depth by price level.
- `L3`: Order-level messages such as adds, cancels, replaces, and executions.
- `q_plus`: Ask-side visible liquidity density.
- `q_minus`: Bid-side visible liquidity density.
- `k`: Market turbulence energy proxy.
- `epsilon`: Market dissipation proxy.
- `nu_t`: Eddy diffusivity proxy defined from `k` and `epsilon`.
- `fragility`: `k^2 / epsilon`, interpreted as persistent volatility under weak dissipation.
- `R_m`: Market Reynolds number proxy for imbalance pressure versus dissipation.
